For quick reference, Diwali is celebrated over 5 days. Relative placement each year:

  • Dhanteras: 2 days before main Diwali
  • Naraka Chaturdashi: day before Diwali
  • Lakshmi Puja: main Diwali day
  • Govardhan Puja: day after Diwali
  • Bhai Dooj: 2 days after Diwali
